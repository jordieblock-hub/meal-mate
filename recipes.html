Tomato Miso Pasta w/ Shrimp

One of those recipes you'll be repeating for a long, long time. Find quality cherry tomatoes and you absolutely cannot go wrong. So simple and so good.

Quick & Easy High Protein

Ingredients:

🍅
Cherry tomatoes 2 pints vine-on
🦐
Shrimp 3/4 lb, peeled
🍝
Thin spaghetti 2 servings
🧄
Garlic 3 cloves, chopped
🧅
Shallots 2 small, sliced
🫙
Miso 1 Tbsp
🫒
Olive oil 1/4 cup
🍋
Lemon juice 1/4 lemon
🌶️
Red pepper flake 1 tsp
🧈
Butter 1 Tbsp
🌿
Parsley finely chopped
🍝 Finished dish

Instructions:

  1. 1Add olive oil to a pan over medium heat, then add cherry tomatoes and season generously with salt.
  2. 2Let tomatoes cook for about 30 seconds, then add sliced shallots and chopped garlic.
  3. 3Meanwhile, bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve pasta water before draining.
  4. 4Cook tomatoes down on medium-low heat until they burst and break down and the shallots are soft, about 10–12 minutes.
  5. 5Add shrimp to the pan and stir to coat in the tomato sauce.
  6. 6Stir in miso, lemon juice, chili flake, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  7. 7Cook for a few more minutes until shrimp are pink and cooked through, then add butter and a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  8. 8Toss in the cooked pasta noodles and fresh parsley, mixing until everything is well coated. Serve immediately.

Miso Mushroom Pasta

So addicting and fully vegetarian, this pasta uses miso and a touch of cream for a rich flavor that compliments a medley of mushrooms. Serves 2.

Vegetarian Budget-Friendly

Ingredients:

🍄
Oyster mushrooms 3 oz
🍄
Shimeji mushrooms 3 oz
🍝
Gemelli pasta 2 servings
🧄
Garlic 2 cloves
🧅
Shallot 1 small
🫙
Miso 1 Tbsp
🥛
Heavy cream 1/4 cup
🍷
White wine 1/3 cup
🧀
Parmesan 1/4 cup grated
🧈
Butter 2 Tbsp
🍋
Lemon juice of 1/4
🌿
Parsley for garnish
🍄 Finished dish

Instructions:

  1. 1Pull mushrooms into large chunks and trim stems. Mince garlic and finely dice shallots.
  2. 2Boil pasta in generously salted water until al dente. Reserve at least 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
  3. 3Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shallots and garlic, sautéing until softened and fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  4. 4Add mushrooms to the pan, spreading in a single layer. Let sit undisturbed for 2–3 minutes to brown evenly, then stir.
  5. 5Deglaze with white wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Stir in miso until fully dissolved.
  6. 6Add cream, lemon juice, parmesan, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. 7Add cooked pasta with 1/4 cup pasta water, then mix vigorously until the sauce clings to every noodle. Plate with extra parmesan and fresh parsley.

Garlic Butter Rice Bowl

The ultimate budget meal that never gets old. Perfectly seasoned garlic butter rice topped with a crispy fried egg and whatever veggies you have on hand.

Budget-Friendly Quick & Easy Vegetarian

Ingredients:

🍚
Rice 1 cup, any variety
🧄
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
🧈
Butter 2 Tbsp
🥚
Eggs 2 large
🥦
Broccoli 1 head, florets
🥕
Carrots 2, julienned
🧂
Soy sauce 2 Tbsp
🌿
Green onions for garnish
🌶️
Sesame oil 1 tsp
🍋
Lime 1, wedged
🍳 Finished dish

Instructions:

  1. 1Cook rice according to package directions. Fluff with a fork when done and set aside.
  2. 2In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add minced garlic and fry, stirring often, until golden and fragrant, about 2–3 minutes.
  3. 3Add broccoli florets and julienned carrots. Sauté until tender-crisp, about 4–5 minutes. Season with a pinch of salt.
  4. 4Push veggies to the side of the pan. Crack eggs into the empty space and fry to your preferred doneness — runny yolks are encouraged.
  5. 5Add cooked rice to the pan and toss everything together with the garlic butter, making sure the rice gets evenly coated.
  6. 6Season with soy sauce and sesame oil, toss once more, then serve topped with sliced green onions and a lime wedge on the side.
